About Me

Hi, I'm Katie Kosaya. I love all kinds of photography, but I am particularly passionate about making food look great. I also love experimenting with old film and polaroid cameras. It's not uncommon to see me carrying 2-3 different cameras when I'm out and about.

You can see more of my personal/lifestyle photography at my blog.

I was born in Ukraine and grew up in Russia, but I currently live in Sandpoint, Idaho with my husband Ben and our little black bunny, Sonya.

I enjoy traveling, and I love food. A lot.
